/ / / / / ABOUT ME

I currently reside in a small suburb in Northwest Indiana. Since graduating college, roughly 14 years ago, I've either mastered or spent an indepth amount of time in every fact of advertising.


My schooling was centered more around media, but I loved building website, primarily in director (then Flash). My first job was to build Flash website for $5 per hour with an extra $5 for every website created. In those days, I could literally churn out 3 websites in an 8 hour day.


Obviously, times have changed- flash is no longer used, making 3 websites a day is pretty insane and the minimum wage rate increased. Soon after, I became a full-time freelance graphic designer, myspace page editor, website designer and local photographer.


I moved away from design for a job at a local casino. After 5 years, I found a position back in graphic design. After a few years in my position and hard lessons, I was rewarded as the Manager of the Advertising Department.


After 9 years (in total), I deciced to take my Advertising knowledge and hands-on approach, into my own company. The timing was maybe the worst, as COVID-19 was making it's way into our news cycle.


I found an opportunity with local bank, in their Corporate Marketing Department, doing motion graphics and cinematography. I greatly enjoy what I do, but I continue to have ideas of larger scale that require more knowledge in the full stack development sector. Hence, here I am.
